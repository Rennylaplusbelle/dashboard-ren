📈 R/R & POT%

POT% (Potential Upside)
POT% = (Target - Entry) / Entry × 100
Entry $100, Target $130 → POT = +30%
R/R (Risk/Reward)
R/R = (Target - Entry) / (Entry - Stop)
Entry $100, Target $130, Stop $90
R/R = 30/10 = 3.0 (gain $3 per $1 risked)
